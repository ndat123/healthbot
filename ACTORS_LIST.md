## 2.1.3. Actors List

The system has three primary actors: User, Doctor, and Admin.

### User

Individuals who use the platform to manage their health, receive medical consultation, and connect with healthcare professionals.

**Roles:**
- Register and log in via Email with password authentication and password recovery.
- Create, update, and manage personal health profiles including age, gender, height, weight, medical history, allergies, and health goals.
- Organize health data into comprehensive profiles and track health metrics over time.
- Search for medical information using the knowledge base and FAQ system.
- Generate personalized health plans and nutrition plans using AI based on their health profile.
- Interact with AI through consultation sessions to receive medical advice and health recommendations.
- Connect and book appointments with doctors through the platform.
- Chat with doctors for post-consultation follow-up and medical questions.
- Track health metrics such as weight, blood pressure, blood sugar, heart rate, and body temperature.
- Record health journals with symptoms, food diary, exercise log, and mood tracking.
- Manage reminders for medications, health checkups, and health-related activities.
- Bookmark and save useful medical articles and health information.

**Goals:**
- Manage personal health efficiently and proactively.
- Access medical consultation 24/7 through AI-powered services.
- Receive personalized health and nutrition plans tailored to individual needs.
- Track health trends and detect potential issues early.
- Connect with qualified doctors when professional consultation is needed.
- Maintain comprehensive health records in one centralized location.

### Doctor

Medical professionals who provide consultation services, manage appointments, and communicate with patients through the platform.

**Roles:**
- Access doctor dashboard to view patient information and appointment schedules.
- View list of patients who have booked appointments or sent messages.
- Manage appointment calendar and availability.
- Respond to patient messages and provide medical advice through the messaging system.
- Update personal information including specialization, education, certifications, and available hours.
- Review patient health profiles and consultation history when providing services.

**Goals:**
- Provide quality medical consultation and follow-up care to patients.
- Manage appointments efficiently and reduce administrative burden.
- Maintain effective communication with patients.
- Build professional reputation through patient reviews and ratings.
- Access patient health information to provide better care.

### Admin

Internal staff responsible for managing the platform's health, safety, content, and performance.

**Roles:**
- Manage the user list including creating, updating, deleting, and locking user accounts.
- Manage doctor accounts and information to ensure qualified professionals are on the platform.
- Review and manage medical content including knowledge base articles, FAQs, and health topics.
- Moderate inappropriate content and ensure medical information accuracy.
- Configure and manage AI settings including API keys, prompts, and training scenarios.
- Monitor AI consultation logs and feedback to improve system performance.
- Review and handle system security issues and user reports.
- Monitor system statistics, analytics, and performance data.
- Generate and download reports on system usage, user engagement, and platform growth.
- Manage system settings, notifications, and deployment configurations.
- View system logs and troubleshoot technical issues.

**Goals:**
- Maintain a safe, secure, and reliable platform for all users.
- Ensure medical content is accurate, up-to-date, and helpful.
- Optimize AI performance and user experience.
- Monitor platform growth and user engagement trends.
- Ensure the system runs smoothly and efficiently.
- Protect user privacy and sensitive health data.
- Comply with healthcare data protection regulations.
